================================================================================
               SERVICE & ADDITIONAL SERVICE IMPLEMENTATION PLAN
================================================================================

PROBLEM: Services and additional services currently increase the order price
but have zero operational/fulfillment logic behind them. This plan defines
10 main service categories with concrete operational impacts, plus 10
subcategories per category.

================================================================================
MAIN CATEGORIES
================================================================================

1. DELIVERY SPEED
   Impact: Determines courier priority, routing logic, and estimated delivery.
   - Express: Priority courier dispatch, same/next-day delivery, fastest route.
   - Standard: Regular queue, 3-5 day delivery, normal routing.
   - Economy: Batched shipping, 5-7 day delivery, consolidated loads.

2. CARGO HANDLING
   Impact: Determines how cargo is processed at warehouses and during transit.
   - Standard handling: Normal loading/unloading, no special care.
   - Careful handling: Labeled "FRAGILE", separate storage, manual loading.
   - Heavy handling: Requires forklift/crane, special loading team.

3. INSURANCE & PROTECTION
   Impact: Creates insurance records and determines compensation on damage/loss.
   - Basic insurance: Covers up to declared_value * 50%, no additional docs.
   - Full insurance: Covers 100% declared_value, requires photo documentation.
   - Premium insurance: Covers declared_value + 20%, includes inspection report.

4. DOOR-TO-DOOR DELIVERY
   Impact: Triggers courier dispatch to recipient address instead of branch pickup.
   - Standard door delivery: Courier delivers during business hours (9-18).
   - Express door delivery: Priority courier, delivery within 2-hour window.
   - Scheduled door delivery: Courier dispatches on user-selected date/time.

5. PACKAGING SERVICES
   Impact: Determines what packaging materials and labor are applied at warehouse.
   - Basic packaging: Standard cardboard box, bubble wrap.
   - Reinforced packaging: Double-wall box, foam inserts, corner protectors.
   - Custom packaging: Tailored packaging for odd-shaped or oversized cargo.

6. TEMPERATURE CONTROL
   Impact: Routes cargo through cold-chain logistics, temperature monitoring.
   - Refrigerated (2-8C): Perishable goods, medical supplies, food.
   - Frozen (-18C and below): Frozen food, biological samples.
   - Climate controlled (15-25C): Electronics, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics.

7. DOCUMENTATION & CUSTOMS
   Impact: Generates required documents, customs declarations, and export permits.
   - Commercial invoice: Generates commercial invoice for B2B shipments.
   - Customs declaration: Prepares and files customs paperwork for cross-border.
   - Export permit: Coordinates with authorities for restricted goods export.

8. TRACKING & NOTIFICATIONS
   Impact: Controls what tracking features and notification channels are active.
   - Basic tracking: Status updates visible in user dashboard only.
   - SMS tracking: Real-time SMS notifications on status changes.
   - GPS tracking: Live map location for in-transit cargo, API integration.

9. STORAGE & WAREHOUSING
   Impact: Determines if cargo is stored at warehouse and for how long.
   - Standard storage: Up to 48h free at origin warehouse before dispatch.
   - Extended storage: Up to 7 days at origin, daily fee after 48h.
   - Climate storage: Temperature-controlled storage until dispatch.

10. SPECIAL CARGO
    Impact: Triggers special handling protocols, restrictions, and compliance.
    - Hazardous materials: DG classification, special labeling, restricted routes.
    - High-value cargo: Secure transport, armed escort, sealed container.
    - Live animals: IATA compliance, ventilation, feeding schedule, vet check.

================================================================================
SUBCATEGORIES
================================================================================

1. DELIVERY SPEED — Subcategories

   1.1 Night delivery
       Impact: Courier dispatches between 18:00-06:00, requires night-shift team.
   1.2 Weekend delivery
       Impact: Delivery on Saturday/Sunday, requires weekend on-call courier pool.
   1.3 Holiday delivery
       Impact: Delivery on public holidays, premium courier assignment.
   1.4 Same-day delivery
       Impact: Order must be placed before 12:00, immediate courier dispatch.
   1.5 Next-day delivery
       Impact: Guaranteed next calendar day, priority queue at all warehouses.
   1.6 Delivery time window
       Impact: User selects 2-hour window (e.g., 10:00-12:00), schedule locked.
   1.7 Multi-stop delivery
       Impact: One cargo delivered to multiple addresses, route optimization.
   1.8 Return delivery
       Impact: Reverse logistics, courier picks up return from recipient.
   1.9 Liftgate delivery
       Impact: Heavy cargo requires truck with hydraulic lift, special vehicle.
   1.10 White glove delivery
        Impact: Inside-delivery, unpacking, assembly, debris removal.

2. CARGO HANDLING — Subcategories

   2.1 Stack protection
       Impact: "DO NOT STACK" labels, top-load storage only.
   2.2 Moisture protection
       Impact: Wrapped in plastic/foil, stored in dry area, humidity monitor.
   2.3 Anti-static handling
       Impact: ESD-safe packaging for electronics, grounded containers.
   2.4 Vibration protection
       Impact: Shock-absorbing inserts for sensitive instruments/equipment.
   2.5 Unpacking and inspection
       Impact: Warehouse opens cargo, inspects contents, logs condition.
   2.6 Palletization
       Impact: Cargo consolidated on pallets, stretch-wrapped, forklift-ready.
   2.7 Crating
       Impact: Custom wooden crate built for fragile or high-value items.
   2.8 Labeling and marking
       Impact: Custom labels, barcodes, QR codes applied per user request.
   2.9 Weight verification
       Impact: Re-weigh cargo at origin and destination, discrepancy report.
   2.10 Photo documentation
        Impact: Photos taken at pickup, each checkpoint, and delivery.

3. INSURANCE & PROTECTION — Subcategories

   3.1 Damage-only coverage
       Impact: Insurance pays only on confirmed physical damage to cargo.
   3.2 Loss coverage
       Impact: Insurance pays on confirmed loss (cargo not delivered at all).
   3.3 Theft coverage
       Impact: Insurance pays on confirmed theft during transit or storage.
   3.4 Water damage coverage
       Impact: Separate policy for water/rain damage during transport.
   3.5 Natural disaster coverage
       Impact: Coverage for force majeure events (flood, earthquake, fire).
   3.6 Delay compensation
       Impact: Penalty paid to recipient if delivery exceeds promised ETA.
   3.7 Proof of condition
       Impact: Mandatory pre-shipment photo report for insurance claim validity.
   3.8 Deductible waiver
       Impact: Higher premium removes customer deductible on claims.
   3.9 Third-party claim handling
       Impact: Insurance company handles claims directly, reduces admin burden.
   3.10 Real-time valuation
        Impact: Cargo value tracked live, premium adjusts if value changes.

4. DOOR-TO-DOOR DELIVERY — Subcategories

   4.1 Floor delivery
       Impact: Courier delivers to specific floor, not just building entrance.
   4.2 Room-of-choice delivery
       Impact: Courier carries cargo to user-specified room inside building.
   4.3 Assembly service
       Impact: Courier assembles furniture/equipment after delivery.
   4.4 Old item removal
       Impact: Courier removes old item when delivering replacement.
   4.5 Scheduled re-delivery
       Impact: If recipient not home, auto-reschedule for next available slot.
   4.6 Neighbor delivery
       Impact: If recipient absent, deliver to specified neighbor with consent.
   4.7 Photo proof of delivery
       Impact: Courier photographs delivered cargo at doorstep as proof.
   4.8 Signature capture
       Impact: Digital signature collected from recipient on delivery.
   4.9 Delivery to rural/remote area
       Impact: Extended delivery zone, additional fee for remote routing.
   4.10 Cross-dock delivery
        Impact: Cargo transferred between vehicles at hub, no warehouse storage.

5. PACKAGING SERVICES — Subcategories

   5.1 Vacuum sealing
       Impact: Air removed from packaging, reduces volume for cost savings.
   5.2 Waterproof wrapping
       Impact: Cargo wrapped in waterproof membrane, sealed seams.
   5.3 Shock-absorbing foam
       Impact: Custom-cut foam inserts shaped to cargo contour.
   5.4 Corner and edge protectors
       Impact: Cardboard/foam protectors on all corners and edges.
   5.5 Pallet wrapping
       Impact: Cargo secured to pallet with shrink-wrap and strapping.
   5.6 Crating service
       Impact: Wooden crate custom-built to cargo dimensions.
   5.7 Labeling service
       Impact: Shipping labels, handling labels, and barcode stickers applied.
   5.8 Unpacking at destination
       Impact: Courier unpacks cargo and removes packaging materials.
   5.9 Fragile item handling
       Impact: "FRAGILE" stickers, separate compartment, manual handling.
   5.10 Oversized cargo packaging
        Impact: Cargo exceeding 150cm requires special crating and handling.

6. TEMPERATURE CONTROL — Subcategories

   6.1 Temperature logger
       Impact: Digital thermometer placed inside, logs every 15 min, report generated.
   6.2 Ice pack provisioning
       Impact: Frozen gel packs added to maintain temperature during transit.
   6.3 Dry ice handling
       Impact: Dry ice added for ultra-cold items, ventilation requirements.
   6.4 Temperature alarm
       Impact: Alert triggered if temperature deviates ±2C from target range.
   6.5 Pre-cooling
       Impact: Cargo refrigerated to target temp before dispatch.
   6.6 insulated container
       Impact: Cargo placed in thermal-lined container for transit.
   6.7 Cold chain handoff
       Impact: Temperature-controlled transfer between vehicles, no ambient exposure.
   6.8 Real-time temp monitoring
       Impact: Dashboard shows live temperature, historical data available.
   6.9 Temperature certificate
       Impact: Official certificate issued at delivery confirming chain was maintained.
   6.10 Frozen cargo unloading
        Impact: Specialized team handles frozen cargo at destination warehouse.

7. DOCUMENTATION & CUSTOMS — Subcategories

   7.1 Packing list
       Impact: Detailed packing list generated from order cargo description.
   7.2 Certificate of origin
       Impact: Official document certifying country where cargo was manufactured.
   7.3 Phytosanitary certificate
       Impact: Required for plant/food products, confirms pest-free status.
   7.4 Dangerous goods declaration
       Impact: DG declaration form generated for hazardous materials.
   7.5 Letter of credit documents
       Impact: Banking documents prepared for international trade payment.
   7.6 ATA Carnet
       Impact: Temporary import/export document for trade shows, samples.
   7.7 Customs bond
       Impact: Financial guarantee filed with customs for cargo release.
   7.8 Import license coordination
       Impact: System checks if import license required, notifies user.
   7.9 HS code classification
       Impact: Correct tariff code assigned to cargo for customs duty calculation.
   7.10 Diplomatic/courier pouch
        Impact: Special handling for embassy/government shipments, expedited customs.

8. TRACKING & NOTIFICATIONS — Subcategories

   8.1 Email notifications
       Impact: Automated emails on each status change (confirmed, in_transit, etc.).
   8.2 Push notifications
       Impact: Mobile/web push notifications for real-time status updates.
   8.3 WhatsApp/Viber integration
       Impact: Status messages sent via messaging apps for wider reach.
   8.4 Estimated time updates
       Impact: ETA recalculated and pushed to user when conditions change.
   8.5 Delivery photo feed
       Impact: User sees live photo updates from warehouse and delivery.
   8.6 Driver contact info
       Impact: Courier phone number visible to recipient during last-mile.
   8.7 Delivery instructions relay
       Impact: User-submitted instructions relayed to courier via system.
   8.8 Delivery confirmation with photo
       Impact: Photo of delivered cargo automatically sent to sender.
   8.9 Analytics dashboard
       Impact: Historical delivery data, on-time rate, avg transit time shown.
   8.10 API webhook
        Impact: External system receives webhook on each status change.

9. STORAGE & WAREHOUSING — Subcategories

   9.1 Pre-dispatch holding
       Impact: Cargo held at origin warehouse until dispatch date.
   9.2 Post-delivery hold
       Impact: Cargo held at destination warehouse for recipient pickup.
   9.3 Consolidation
       Impact: Multiple orders from same sender consolidated into single shipment.
   9.4 Deconsolidation
       Impact: Combined shipment split into individual orders at destination hub.
   9.5 Cross-docking
       Impact: Cargo transferred directly between inbound/outbound vehicles.
   9.6 Inventory management
       Impact: Warehouse tracks cargo quantity and location in real-time.
   9.7 Order picking
       Impact: Warehouse staff picks cargo from shelves for dispatch.
   9.8 Staging area
       Impact: Cargo grouped in staging zone by route before loading.
   9.9 Returns processing
       Impact: Warehouse receives, inspects, and restocks returned cargo.
   9.10 Overflow storage
        Impact: Temporary storage at partner facility when main warehouse full.

10. SPECIAL CARGO — Subcategories

    10.1 Live animal transport
         Impact: Ventilated crate, feeding schedule, vet inspection, IATA LAR compliance.
    10.2 Pharmaceutical transport
         Impact: GDP compliance, temperature log, chain of custody documentation.
    10.3 Art and antiques
         Impact: Custom crating, white-glove handling, climate-controlled transit.
    10.4 Jewelry and precious metals
         Impact: Sealed container, armed escort option, GPS tracking, vault storage.
    10.5 Firearms and ammunition
         Impact: Legal documentation, locked container, restricted route, age verification.
    10.6 Electronics and semiconductors
         Impact: Anti-static packaging, humidity control, shock monitoring.
    10.7 Chemical and industrial
         Impact: Hazmat labeling, SDS documentation, spill kit on vehicle.
    10.8 Food and beverages
         Impact: Cold chain, expiration date tracking, FIFO dispatch, food safety cert.
    10.9 Automotive parts
         Impact: Oil/fluid leak protection, heavy-item handling, crane loading.
    10.10 Construction materials
         Impact: Flatbed vehicle, oversized load permits, route clearance.

================================================================================
IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
================================================================================

To make these categories functional (not just pricing), the system needs:

A. SERVICE ACTION MODELS
   - Create a ServiceAction model that maps each service/subcategory to a
     concrete action type and parameters (e.g., dispatch_courier, create_insurance_record,
     notify_warehouse, generate_document).

B. ORDER LIFECYCLE HOOKS
   - On order creation: create all ServiceAction records from selected services.
   - On status change: check ServiceAction records, execute pending actions.
   - On delivery: mark ServiceAction records as completed.

C. OPERATIONAL INTEGRATIONS
   - Warehouse staff dashboard: shows pending actions per order (packaging, storage).
   - Courier dispatch: door-to-door services trigger courier assignment.
   - Document generation: documentation services trigger report creation.
   - Insurance system: insurance services create policy records.

D. PRIORITY QUEUE
   - Delivery speed determines order priority in warehouse and courier dispatch.
   - Express orders processed first, Economy orders batched.

E. NOTIFICATION SYSTEM
   - Each service subcategory can trigger notifications (email, SMS, push).
   - Warehouse staff notified of handling requirements.
   - Recipient notified of delivery type and ETA.
